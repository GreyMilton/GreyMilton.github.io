About me

Hello and welcome!

My name is Grey Milton. I am newly trained full-stack software developer, who has recently graduated from an industry leading coding bootcamp at Northcoders. I have created this portfolio website to showcase some of the coding projects I have completed over the past few months.

Originally a musician by trade, I have long had strengths in mathematics and the visual arts, with a keen eye and disposition to fine details. I initally dipped my feet into coding when helping my wife with her online business, by designing and tweaking the CSS for two of her websites: gettingnaked.com.au and yogaforthevagina.com.

In a fortunate string of events, I found my way to the bootcamp course on the advice of a lifelong friend and coder, and I have now learnt a vast array of new skills and concepts including JavaScript, React, React Native, RESTful APIs, TDD with Jest and Supertest, PostgreSQL and much more.

I am excited to be embarking on this new career in Software Development, soaking up any chance I can to learn and grow my skillset.

I invite you to browse my portfolio website, and check out the GitHub repos for each portfolio piece, for a more in depth look at my work. If you have any questions or comments on what you find, feel free to contact me.

All the best,
Grey
